# Shopping Cart - User Guide

## How It Works

### Adding Products to Cart

1. **Browse Products**
   - Go to the Search page or Homepage
   - Browse through available building materials
   - View product details, prices, and stock availability

2. **Add to Cart**
   - Click the "Add to Cart" button on any product card
   - You'll see a success notification in the top-right corner
   - The cart icon in the header will update with the item count
   - The cart drawer will automatically open to show your addition

3. **Add Multiple Items**
   - Continue shopping and add more products
   - The cart badge shows total items (not total products)
   - Adding the same product twice increases the quantity

### Viewing Your Cart

#### Quick View (Cart Drawer)

1. **Click the Cart Icon** in the header (top-right)
2. A slide-out drawer appears showing:
   - All items in your cart
   - Product images and names
   - Supplier information
   - Quantity controls
   - Per-item prices
   - Running totals

#### Full Cart Page

1. **Click "Proceed to Checkout"** in the cart drawer, OR
2. **Navigate directly** to `/cart`
3. See expanded view with:
   - Larger product cards
   - Detailed supplier information
   - Stock availability
   - Eco ratings and certifications
   - Complete order summary

### Managing Cart Items

#### Change Quantity
- Use the **+** button to increase quantity
- Use the **-** button to decrease quantity
- Quantity is limited by available stock
- Cart totals update automatically

#### Remove Items
- Click the **trash icon** on any item
- Item is immediately removed
- Cart recalculates totals

#### Clear Entire Cart
- Click "Clear Cart" button (top-right on cart page)
- Confirms removal of all items
- Use with caution!

### Checkout Process

1. **Review Cart**
   - Check all items and quantities
   - Verify supplier selections
   - Review order total

2. **Proceed to Checkout**
   - Click "Proceed to Checkout" button
   - Navigate to checkout page
   - Enter shipping and payment details

3. **Complete Order**
   - Submit payment information
   - Receive order confirmation
   - Get email with order details

### Cart Persistence

Your cart is automatically saved:
- **Persists across browser sessions**
- **Survives page refreshes**
- **Available when you return**
- **Stored locally on your device**

## Troubleshooting

**Cart not updating?**
- Refresh the page
- Check browser console for errors
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled

**Items disappeared?**
- Check if you cleared browser data
- Cart persists in localStorage
- Re-add items if lost

**Can't add more items?**
- Stock limit reached
- Supplier doesn't have enough quantity
- Try a different supplier

**Wrong totals?**
- Refresh cart page
- Check quantities are correct
- Verify VAT is included (20%)

## Mobile Usage

The cart works seamlessly on mobile:

- **Cart icon** visible in header (hamburger menu area)
- **Full-screen cart drawer** for easy viewing
- **Touch-friendly** buttons and controls
- **Swipe gestures** (close drawer by clicking outside)
