1. **Product Name**: 1.125rem (18px), Bold, Dark text

3. **Description**: 0.9375rem (15px), Regular, Gray
4. **Meta Info**: 0.875rem (14px), Medium, Gray
5. **Badges**: 0.75rem (12px), Bold, Colored backgrounds
